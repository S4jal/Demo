Tank vs. Tankless Water Heaters: What's The Difference?

Tank

  • Stores Water Internally
  • Lower Initial Cost
  • Simple Installation
  • Less Time For Water To Heat Up
VS

Tankless

  • Heats Up Water On Demand
  • More Energy Efficient
  • Compact Design
  • Longer Life Span
