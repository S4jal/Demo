Tank vs. Tankless

Traditional Tank

  • Lower upfront cost
  • Simple, proven technology
  • Stores 40–80 gallons of hot water
  • ~10–12 year lifespan
  • Takes up more floor space

Tankless

  • Endless on-demand hot water
  • Lower monthly energy bills
  • Compact — frees up space
  • ~20+ year lifespan
  • Higher upfront, bigger long-term savings
